Elements


			Elements are designators that define the structure and content of objects

			 within a page. Some of the more frequently used elements include multiple 

			levels of headings (identified as h1 through h6 elements) and paragraphs 

			(identified as the p element); the list goes on to include the a, div, 

			span, strong, and em elements, and many more.

			Elements are identified by the use of less-than and greater-than angle 
			
			brackets, < >, surrounding the element name.
